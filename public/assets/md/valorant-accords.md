# THE VALORANT ACCORDS

1. it&rsquo;s dangerous to go alone, always bring a friend
     - **[exception 1]** if it&rsquo;s not [Competitive](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Competitive), whatever floats your boat
2. warming up is required for all party members
     - **[note 1]** the following [Game Modes](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Game_Modes) are valid for warming up
          - [Unrated](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Unrated)
          - [Swiftplay](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Swiftplay)
          - [All Random One Site](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/All_Random_One_Site)
          - [Replication](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Replication)
          - New Map
          - [Deathmatch](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Deathmatch)
          - [Escalation](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Escalation)
          - [Team Deathmatch](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Team_Deathmatch)
          - [Skirmish](https://breezewiki.com/valorant/wiki/Skirmish)
3. once RR is net positive, you&rsquo;re done
     - **[note 1]** this is based on the player with the lowest rank and then least RR
     - **[exception 1]** if playing another game could lead to a rank up, go for it
     - **[exception 2]** if playing another game could lead to a rank down, stop immediately
     - **[exception 3]** guests may veto this rule, since we want them to have fun
